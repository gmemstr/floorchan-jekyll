These are the perks and benefits you can get when you donate the amount specified. You can donate to recieve multiple perks at once. Perks are good for either one year if you pay the yearly due, or are good permanently for your account if you pay the permanent price. 3-day trials of any and all features are available upon request by emailing the admin.

Here are the perks available to you for purchase.
 
Perk Yearly Price Permanent Price Notes
+512MB maximum memory for your Minecraft server 5 USD / year 10 USD Stackable up to a maximum quantity of 2 for a total of 2GB of RAM. Please email the admin for more RAM usage beyond 2GB.
Subdomain for your website 1 USD / year 2 USD

Your subdomain can be *.floorchan.org except for certain reserved subdomains. Your subdomain will use your public_html directory. 
"Bring Your Own Domain" for your website 1 USD / year 2 USD Your domain will run under a Apache2 Virtual Host out of your own public_html directory. Discounts may be available if you allow other Floorchan users to make subdomains using your domain name. Contact the admin for details.
Managed Domain for your website 10 USD / year + cost of domain registration and yearly renewal 50 USD + cost of domain registration and yearly renewal Your domain will run under a Apache2 Virtual Host out of your own public_html directory. Unless otherwise specified, when possible, the domain will be hosted at Namecheap.com. If you wish to take ownership of the domain, domain transfers will be possible.
One extra listening TCP and UDP port for your application 1 USD / year 5 USD Ports are available on a first come, first served basis. Obtaining a port number grants you listening rights on that port for both TCP and UDP. Ports are assigned from the range of 1025-40000 on our 4 different IPs.
Removal of Floorchan branding from your game server 1 USD / year 2 USD If the admin sets up a game server for you, they will include Floorchan branding in the MOTD or server title (varies by game, ask admin for details) stating "Powered by floorchan.org.".
Donations are handled via Paypal or BitGo (depending on payment method) and are sent to camoyoshi@trioptimum.com. Perks should be applied to your account within one week after the payment clears. Please note that we cannot offer refunds. Make sure you make the most of your 3-day trial before donating for a perk and test what you can. When you donate, please let us know if you want to be listed in the donor list below, along with your amount, or if you prefer to be listed as anonymous. We currently accept the following donation methods:
  • Paypal (One-time or Subcription)
  • Bitcoin
