--- title: Services layout: default ---

We provide various public services for our members to take advantage of:

SSH accounts

On our server, we run Debian Testing x64 on a Intel-Xeon based platform. We have various shells available including sh, ksh, csh, and bash, along with their respective variants. We also provide the following utilities that any user can use:

  • Text editors/modifiers: vi, emacs, nano, grep, sed, awk...
  • Development tools: python, perl, gcc, g++, git...
  • Network tools: ping, traceroute, dig, wget, ftp, scp, ssh, rsync, nc, curl, links, whois, aria2...
  • Games: bsdgames, nethack-console, sudoku, nimvaders, greed
  • Communication: irssi, weechat
  • Console management: screen, tmux
  • File management: mc
 
These quotas can be increased by donations; please read this page for more information.

Free Web Hosting

We also have free web hosting for your own personal webpages, which are served via Apache2 w/ PHP. We do not provide databases of any kind. Users will upload their website's files via SCP/SSH-FTP. (We do not run our FTP to allow uploading from users). Your site address will be http://users.floorchan.org/~your-login-username/.

Free Email Accounts

We also offer free email accounts with webmail access via SquirrelMail!

Various Game Servers

We also run a few public game servers for anyone to play on, and a few private servers for certain members only. You can view our publicly available servers by going to http://games.floorchan.org/.
